This project investigates how new digital technologies can support the musical practice of Bra-
zilian choro. Our research combines musicological expertise and cultural heritage research with
computational methods for large-scale music analysis and the development of AI-driven tools for
harmonic analysis and automatic harmonization of choro melodies.

[Click here to read more about the project](https://fabianmoss.github.io/choro/about/)
